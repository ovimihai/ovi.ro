<p>CheatSheets are pieces of paper that sum up some importat aspects of a topic.</p>

<a href="https://www.cheatography.com/davechild/cheat-sheets/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cheatography.com</a> seems to be the biggest searchable cheatsheets, but also in pdf format, literally hundreds</li>
